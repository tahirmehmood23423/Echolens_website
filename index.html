FAQ

Questions, answered honestly.

01Do I need a background in coding or maths?+

For bootcamps and short courses, no — we build the foundation with you. For specialist and career tracks, comfort with basic Python helps, and we'll tell you honestly on the intake call whether you should take a foundational course first.

02Are classes live or recorded?+

All classes are live — instructor-led, two hours, twice a week. Sessions are recorded so you can rewatch, but the live cohort is where the real work happens.

03What if I miss a class?+

Recordings are made available the same day. For unavoidable absences, we run a make-up session on request — that's part of being a small cohort.

04What does the certificate actually mean?+

It is issued only on completion of real work — not attendance. It lists the projects you shipped, the stack you used, and the capstone you defended. Designed to hold up to a recruiter's reading.

05How do payments work?+

Bank transfer, Easypaisa, or JazzCash — full or in two instalments depending on the program. Receipts arrive within an hour. Refunds available in the first week, less platform fees.

06Can I switch programs after enrolling?+

Yes — within the first week. Talk to us on WhatsApp and we'll move you to a better-fit program or hold your seat for the next cohort.
